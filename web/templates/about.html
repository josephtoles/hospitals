About Us

In 2013, a landmark study estimated patient deaths in American hospitals due to preventable harm at over 210,000 deaths per year1, roughly the number of American soldiers killed in the entire Vietnam War. Many studies put the death toll even higher. Led only by heart disease and cancer, it seems that hospitals are the third leading cause of death in the United States, surpassing stroke, diabetes, Alzheimer’s, and all other forms of accident2. Uncounted other patients are crippled or maimed every day due to preventable infections, medicinal side effects, and unnecessary surgeries.

While the root causes of these issues are undoubtedly complex, we believe patients have a right to information. We aim to make choosing which hospital is best for you as clear as possible, based thousands of datasets made available under the Affordable Care Act. We’ll be with you all the way to the operating room.
